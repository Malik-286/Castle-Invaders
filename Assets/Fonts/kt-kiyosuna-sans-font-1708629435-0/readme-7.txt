Thank you for downloading KT Kiyosuna Sans.
Before starting to use the font file, please read the following instructions:

KT Kiyosuna Sans is the deriving typeface of Zen Kaku Gothic (https://github.com/googlefonts/zen-kakugothic), designed by Yoshimichi Ohira. This font’s Kanji (CJK Unified Ideographs) characters originated from Zen Kaku Gothic. I would like to express my deepest gratitude to every person related to producing the font.

KT Kiyosuna Sans is available under the SIL Open Font License 1.1. For further information about the license, please see enclosed OFL.txt and https://scripts.sil.org/OFL.
